Commands

All available commands for WondeX. Prefix: Wa!

🔨 Moderation

kick
Wa!kick <member> [reason]

Kick a member from the server.

Kick Members
ban
Wa!ban <member> [reason]

Permanently ban a member from the server.

Ban Members
unban
Wa!unban <user#tag>

Unban a member by their username#discriminator.

Ban Members
mute
Wa!mute <member> [reason]

Mute a member by assigning the Muted role (creates it if missing).

Manage Roles
unmute
Wa!unmute <member>

Remove the Muted role from a member.

Manage Roles
warn
Wa!warn <member> [reason]

Warn a member via DM and log it in the channel.

Manage Messages
purge
Wa!purge <1–100>

Bulk-delete up to 100 messages from the current channel.

Manage Messages

🛡️ Security

lockdown
Wa!lockdown

Deny @everyone from sending messages in the current channel.

Manage Channels
unlock
Wa!unlock

Restore @everyone message permissions in the current channel.

Manage Channels
serverinfo
Wa!serverinfo

Display info about the server: owner, members, channels, roles, creation date.

Everyone
userinfo
Wa!userinfo [member]

Display info about a member (defaults to yourself).

Everyone

🎫 Tickets

ticketpanel
Wa!ticketpanel

Post the ticket panel with an Open Ticket 🎫 button. Members click it to create a private support channel. Inside the ticket: Close Ticket 🔒 and Claim 👋.

Manage Channels

ℹ️ General

help
Wa!help

Show a summary of all available commands in Discord.

Everyone
